FAQ

Common questions

Still have questions? Get in touch — I'll reply within a few hours.

How long does a website take?

+

Most sites are delivered in 7–14 days depending on the package. Once you're happy with revisions, the site goes live.

What do I need to provide?

+

Your logo (or we can create one), any photos you want to use, and a brief about your business and goals. I handle everything else.

Can I update the site myself?

+

Yes. Sites are built so you can update text and images yourself — or I can do it for you with a maintenance plan.

Do you offer payment plans?

+

Yes — 50% deposit upfront, 50% on completion. Flexible options available for larger projects. Just ask.

What if I'm not happy with the result?

+

Your satisfaction is the priority. We work through revision rounds until you're completely happy before anything goes live.

Do you work with businesses outside the UK?

+

Absolutely. I work with clients globally. All communication is online and timezones have never been an issue.
