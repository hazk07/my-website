FAQ

Common questions

Still have questions? Get in touch and I'll get back to you within a few hours.

How long does a website take to build?

+

Most sites are delivered within 7–14 days depending on the package. Once you're happy and revisions are done, the site is live.

What do I need to provide?

+

Your logo (or we can create one), any photos or images you want to use, and a brief about your business and goals. We handle everything else.

Will I be able to update the site myself?

+

Yes. Sites are built on easy-to-use platforms so you can update text and images yourself — or we can do it for you with a maintenance plan.

Do you offer payment plans?

+

Yes — a 50% deposit upfront and 50% on completion. Flexible options available for larger projects. Just ask.

What if I'm not happy with the result?

+

Your satisfaction is the priority. We work through revision rounds to make sure you're absolutely happy before anything goes live.
